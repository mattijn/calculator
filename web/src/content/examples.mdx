# Worked Examples

These mirror the original prototype explanations.

## Savings growth

Start with 100 and compound 3% for 10 years.

Expression: `100 × 1.03↑10`

## Required principal

How much to save now to have 1000 after 10 years at 3%.

Expression: `1000 ÷ 1.03↑10`

## Rate to double in 10 years

Solve `r` in `r↑10 = 2`.

Expression: `2↓10`

## Years to double at 3%

Solve `y` in `1.03↑y = 2`.

Expression: `2⇓1.03`
