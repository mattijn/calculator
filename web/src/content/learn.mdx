# Learn The Notation

This project explores notation proposed in [Numbers](https://homepages.cwi.nl/~steven/Talks/2019/11-21-dijkstra/Numbers.pdf).

The goal is to make relationships between power, root, and logarithms visually symmetric.

## Core Idea

`a↑b` means power, `a↓b` means the b-th root, and `a⇓b` means log base b. They are direct inverses.

## Why this helps

- Operators look related, so inverse operations are easier to remember.
- Financial or growth formulas become easier to read.
- The same symbolic language can express solve-for-principal, rate, and time variants.
